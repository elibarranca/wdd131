Overview

Purpose

New Students come to the city of Rexburg every 4 months to start their studies at BYUI, and they have many tools at their disposal to learn their way in the University, but there is no similar tool that will teach them about their city and the resources taht are available to them, so I decided to solve this by creating a simple New Student Guide to the beautiful city of Rexburg!

Audience

All the students at Brigham Young University, but specially Freshman.

Dynamic elements

Arrays of the different elements in my website, a form where the user can ask for more information and send their email to be contacted, Events so when the user clicks on a button to show an image it will open a bigger image and a link to a map, Functions to have a responsive menu, and Sorting for the elements that the user wants to see
